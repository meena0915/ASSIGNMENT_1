The dataset consists of 1001 records of students academic performance along with various attributes.It focuses on these various factors and students scores.
gender is categorical data that is has values 'male','female'
race/ethnicity columns indicates student's race or ethnic group belong to 'groupA','groupB','groupC','groupD'
parental level of education column indicates the highest level of education completed by the student's parent(s)
lunch column indicates which type of lunch student is receiving whether it is 'standard' or 'free/reduced'
test preparation course column indicates whether the student completed a test preparation course before the exam.
math course column indicates student's score in the math exam (out of 100).
reading score indicates the student's score in the reading exam (out of 100).
writing score indicates the student's score in the writing exam (out of 100).
